Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

ZeroDebt respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the ZeroDebt application.

1. Information We Collect

ZeroDebt does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to any external servers.

All financial data you enter (balances, payments, installments, goals, etc.) is stored locally on your device.

We do not require account registration. We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Government IDs
  • Bank credentials
  • Card numbers

2. Local Data Storage

All data entered into the app is stored locally on your device using your browser or device storage.

If you uninstall the app or clear your device storage, your data may be permanently deleted.

3. Export Feature

If you use the Export feature (PDF, CSV, JSON), files are generated locally on your device. We do not receive or store exported data.

You are responsible for how you store or share exported files.

4. Internet Usage

The app may require internet access only for:

  • Initial loading (if hosted online)
  • App updates

All financial calculations are performed locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

ZeroDebt does not integrate with:

  • Banks
  • Credit card providers
  • Financial institutions
  • Payment processors

We do not access or retrieve bank data automatically.

6. Data Security

Since data is stored locally, its security depends on your device security settings. We recommend:

  • Using device passcodes
  • Enabling biometric lock
  • Not sharing exported files publicly

7. Children's Privacy

ZeroDebt is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

8. Disclaimer

ZeroDebt provides financial tracking and estimation tools only. Calculations are estimates and may differ from official bank computations. This app does not provide financial advice.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date at the top of this page.
